About Us

Master Tailoring & Repair has been serving Quezon City for nearly a decade. What started as a small passion project has grown into a trusted shop with a dedicated team of skilled tailors who treat every garment with care.

We specialize in wedding and special occasion wear — from bridal gowns and barong tagalogs to debut dresses and suits. We know how much these moments matter, and we make sure every stitch is worthy of them.

Our team brings together years of hands-on experience and a genuine love for the craft. Whether you need a last-minute repair or a fully custom piece, we're here to make sure you look and feel your best.
